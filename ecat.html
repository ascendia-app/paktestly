ECAT Resources

ECAT is the primary entry test for engineering universities in Punjab, conducted by UET Lahore. It is the gatekeeper for seats in UET and several other government-sector engineering institutions. The test consists of Multiple Choice Questions from Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and English. Unlike the NET, ECAT is usually held once a year after the second-year board exams, putting significant pressure on students to perform in a single attempt.
