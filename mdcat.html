MDCAT Resources

MDCAT is the national standardized test for pre-medical students seeking admission to public and private medical and dental colleges across Pakistan. Regulated by PMDC, the test focuses on Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and English at the FSc/A-Level difficulty. It is a high-stakes, paper-based exam known for its immense competition, requiring a deep understanding of concepts and exceptional time management skills.
