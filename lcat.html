LCAT Resources

The LCAT is the in-house entrance exam for the Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS) for those who do not submit SAT scores. It evaluates critical thinking, reading comprehension, and mathematical reasoning. LUMS looks for a holistic profile, and a strong LCAT score is a major component of a successful application.
