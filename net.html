NUST-NET Resources

The NUST Entry Test is a computer-based competitive exam required for admission to various undergraduate programs at NUST. It is conducted in four series (NET 1–4) throughout the year. The test primarily evaluates candidates on Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, English, and Intelligence. NUST considers your best score among all attempted series for the final merit calculation, making it one of the most flexible but challenging engineering entrance exams in Pakistan.
